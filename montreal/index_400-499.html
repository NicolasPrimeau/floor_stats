Montreal

Montréal is the second-largest city in Canada and the largest city in the province of Quebec. Originally called Ville-Marie, or "City of Mary", the city takes its present name from Mont-Royal, the triple-peaked hill located in the heart of the city, whose name was also initially given to the island on which the city is located, or Mont Réal as it was spelled in Middle French, (Mont Royal in present French).

Wikipedia 2010

As of the 2011 Census, Statistics Canada identifies Montreal's Census Metropolitan Area (CMA) (land area 4,258 square kilometres (1,644 sq mi)) as Canada's second most populous with a population of 1,649,519 in the city and metropolitan area population of 3,824,221.
